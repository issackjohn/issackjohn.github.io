00About

I’m a software engineer at Microsoft Edge. Most of the work is less glamorous than it sounds: looking at real workloads, cutting away noise, and making the signal easier to trust.

I spend most of my time on Speedometer 3.0 and the parts of performance work that usually get left out of the story — measurement fidelity, benchmark honesty, and keeping the tooling useful.

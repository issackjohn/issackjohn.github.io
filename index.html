About

I’m a software engineer at Microsoft Edge focused on browser performance and benchmark design. Most of the work is less glamorous than it sounds: looking at real workloads, cutting away noise, and making the signal easier to trust.

My work centers on Speedometer 3.0 and on the practical parts of performance work that usually get left out of the story: measurement fidelity, benchmark honesty, and keeping the tooling useful.
